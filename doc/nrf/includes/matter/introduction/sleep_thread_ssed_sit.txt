This device works as a Matter accessory device, meaning it can be paired and controlled remotely over a Matter network built on top of a low-power 802.15.4 Thread network and works as a :ref:`Minimal Thread Device (MTD) in a Synchronized Sleepy End Device (SSED) variant <thread_ot_device_types>`.
Additionally, the device works as Matter Intermittently Connected Device (ICD) with a :ref:`Short Idle Time (SIT) <ug_matter_device_low_power_icd_sit_lit>`.

You can use this |matter_type| as a reference for creating your own application.
See the :ref:`ug_matter_creating_accessory` page for an overview of the process you need to follow.
