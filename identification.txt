If you test with the development kit, locate the Button 4 and press it shortly.
Observe that LED 4 blinks at a 0.5 second interval to indicate that the identification mode is active.

If you test with the Thingy platform, locate the only button on the device and long press it until you hear two short beeps (3s < press time < 5s).
Observe that RGB LED uses yellow color in its blink sequence to indicate that the identification mode is active.
